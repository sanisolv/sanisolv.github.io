THE NEED FOR CLEAN LIVING

A lack of plumbing infrastructure has left approximately 70% of India with inadequate access to sanitation methods. Current options occupy too much space, are too expensive, and aren't built for removing waste from the environment.

OUR SOLUTION

We are building a waterless, electricity free toilet solution that is affordable to all communities and can help reduce problems like water pollution and sanitation safety concerns for women.

FOSTERING GROWTH

In creating sanitation, SaniSolvr also provides entrepreneurial opportunities for local people to establish new toilet business and stimulate financial growth for themselves and the local economy.
